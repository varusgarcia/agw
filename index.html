A little bit about Me...

Hey there! My Name is Alvaro Garcia Weissenborn and I'm currently studying interface design at the Fh-Potsdam. Living in Potsdam, a nice town at only 20 min. away from Berlin.
I’m originally from Madrid, Spain, where I attended school and High school and basically lived my whole life. I would classify myself as a curious person, always trying out new ways and interested in many different paths, such as cinematography, photography, technology, mountain bike, nature, painting,.. Paths that have, for example, carried me to spend half a year in a farm in England. My interest in technology has driven me to leave my home country one year ago and start studying here in Germany. I got interested in interface design because of its importance in the modern world and because its something alive, evolving every day. One year ago I was introduced to the world of code and since then I have experimented with Processing, Arduino, Javascript, Html, CSS, and jquery. This website is actually my first page which I've coded from scratch. I want it to be a potfolio for my projects and tutorials.
